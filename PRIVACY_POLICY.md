# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 28, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Fanta F1** (also known as "IDGAF-1"), a fantasy Formula 1 management application developed by **Francesco Battaglini** ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our").

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use the Fanta F1 mobile application ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Email address and password** — Used for account creation and authentication via Firebase Authentication.
- **Display name and profile photo** — Set by you during sign-up or later in your account settings.
- **Team name and team avatar** — Created by you for your fantasy F1 team. Team names and avatar images are visible to other players within the same lobby.
- **Lobby name and password** — Created when you set up or join a fantasy lobby.
- **Fantasy lineup selections** — Your driver picks for each race, including driver choices and associated fantasy costs.
- **Scores and team points** — Calculated from your lineup selections and aggregated across races.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device model** — The model name of your device (e.g., "iPhone 15", "Pixel 8") is collected to associate with your push notification registration.
- **Firebase Authentication UID** — A unique identifier generated by Firebase for your account.
- **Push notification tokens** — Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device tokens are stored to deliver push notifications (e.g., race reminders, lineup updates). These tokens are mapped to your device model name.

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- **No analytics or tracking** — We do not use any analytics SDKs (including Firebase Analytics), crash reporting tools, or user behavior tracking.
- **No location data** — The App does not request or collect your GPS location.
- **No device identifiers** — We do not collect IMEI, Android ID, IDFV, IDFA, or any other persistent device identifiers.
- **No contacts, calendar, microphone, or camera access** — The App does not access your contacts, calendar, or microphone. Camera access is only used for capturing photos to use as a team avatar.
- **No advertising or tracking** — No advertising SDKs or third-party tracking services are used.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

1. **Account management** — Authenticating you and maintaining your user profile.
2. **Fantasy gameplay** — Storing and managing your team, lobby, lineup selections, scores, and points.
3. **Push notifications** — Sending race-related notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging (you can opt out at any time).
4. **Team avatars** — Storing uploaded team avatar images in Firebase Storage.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Firebase Services

The App uses **Firebase** (by Google) as its backend infrastructure. Your data is stored in the following Firebase services:

- **Firebase Authentication** — Your email, password (hashed), display name, and photo URL are managed by Firebase Auth.
- **Cloud Firestore** — Your user profile, teams, lobbies, lineups, and scores are stored in Firestore databases.
- **Firebase Storage** — Team avatar images are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage.
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging** — Push notification tokens are managed via FCM.

### 4.2 Security Measures

- Passwords are hashed and stored server-side by Firebase Authentication.
- Access to your data is controlled through Firebase Firestore security rules.
- Team avatar images are stored with user-specific path patterns.

### 4.3 Your Responsibilities

- Keep your login credentials secure.
- Use a strong password for your account.
- Notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

## 5. Data Sharing

We **do not sell, trade, rent, or share** your personal information with any third parties, except as described below:

- **Firebase (Google)** — Your data is stored and processed using Firebase services as described in Section 4. Firebase operates under Google's Privacy Policy.
- **Legal requirements** — We may disclose your data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 6. Data Retention

- Your account data (profile, teams, lobbies, lineups, scores) is retained as long as your account is active.
- If you sign out or delete your account, your data will remain in our Firebase databases until you request its deletion (see Section 8).
- Push notification tokens are updated whenever they are refreshed. Old tokens are overwritten.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access** — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction** — Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
- **Deletion** — Request deletion of your account and associated data.
- **Portability** — Request transfer of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **francesco@battaglini.net**. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

### Deleting Your Account

To delete your account and all associated data:

1. Sign out of the App.
2. Email us at **francesco@battaglini.net** with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
3. We will delete your Firebase Auth account and all associated Firestore documents (user profile, teams, lobbies, lineups, scores) and remove your avatar images from Firebase Storage.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

## 9. Push Notifications

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications. You can opt out of push notifications at any time through your device settings.

- **iOS:** Settings → Notifications → Fanta F1 → Toggle off
- **Android:** Settings → Apps → Fanta F1 → Notifications → Toggle off

Opting out will not affect your ability to use the App's core features.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for the latest information on our data practices.
